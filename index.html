Gardens of Distinction specializes in the landscape design and maintenance of public parks and residential gardens in New York City.

We also provide horticultural services for coop and townhouse gardens, roof gardens, terrace staging, and the integration of plants into interior residential and corporate spaces.

Our design philosophy considers the surrounding buildings and the local environment and climate. We help you choose plants that are right for your site.

We also try to be environmentally responsible with our supplies and tools. All products used are organic and natural and we introduce ongoing composting whenever feasible.

Most importantly, we remain client-focused throughout the design process. Our goal is to help you achieve your garden vision.
