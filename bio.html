Susan Sipos, President

Susan Sipos is the landscape architect for the world famous Jefferson Market Garden and the garden at St.Luke in the Fields in Greenwich Village. Susan's landscapes have been used as backdrops by, among others, "Sex in the City", Stella McCartney's collection openings, the Bonobos fashion shoot, photo shoots for David Duchovny and Netflix and photography by Oberto Gili.

She has special expertise in classical landscape design, water features and the integration of other natural elements like birds, fish and butterflies into a garden.

Susan received her training at the School of Professional Horticultural at the New York Botanical Gardens. She has trained with the New York Audobon Society about the best methods to attract birdlive to gardens. She is an active member of Koi Gardeners of the Northeast.

Susan is a member of The Horticultural Society of New York and has sat on horticultural panels for Greenwich Village Society for Historical Preservation .
