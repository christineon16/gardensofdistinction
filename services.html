Services

Consultation This one-hour process is essential to our collaboration. You should be prepared to find out what plants will work best for you, the extent of maintenance, what your budget will be and the length of the design and installation process.

Design We will take into consideration the kinds of plants and looks you like as we develop a planting plan. We will also work with your coop board if you need their approval for the design.

Installation We take care of the purchase and installation of plants and furniture. We also ensure you are connected to a water supply.

Maintenance We provide regular visits for fertilization, pruning and seasonal plantings. For interior spaces, we handle regular watering and fertilization.
